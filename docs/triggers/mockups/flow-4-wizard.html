When a meeting ends A conferencing app (Zoom, Teams, Meet in a browser…) held your mic for at least 5 minutes, then released it and stayed quiet for ~2 minutes.

How Mnema detects it

Detection is automatic — nothing to configure. Mnema watches which app holds the microphone (the macOS "orange dot" signal); a browser counts when a meeting URL was seen during the call. Drop/rejoin gaps are absorbed.

When an app opens A chosen app comes to the front after you've been away from it for 30 minutes or more — a fresh working session, not window switching.

Which app?

Any moment the app is frontmost resets the 30-minute clock, so rapid switching never fires it.

On a schedule At a fixed local time on the days you pick — good for daily wrap-ups and weekly reviews.
